How does natural language programming work?

Our AI analyzes your description, understands the requirements, and generates clean, functional code. It uses advanced natural language processing to interpret your ideas and translate them into programming constructs.

What programming languages are supported?

We support major programming languages including JavaScript, Python, Java, C#, TypeScript, Go, and more. The AI chooses the best language based on your project requirements.

Is the generated code production-ready?

Yes! Our system generates code that follows industry best practices, includes proper error handling, and is optimized for performance. All applications are automatically tested before deployment.

How much does it cost?

We offer flexible pricing plans starting from free tier for basic usage. Paid plans include more features, higher usage limits, and priority support. Costs are based on compute resources used.

Can I modify the generated code?

Absolutely! You have full access to the generated code. You can download it, modify it, and even redeploy it. The code is yours to own and customize.

Is my data secure?

Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use enterprise-grade security measures and comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

What if I need help with my project?

We provide comprehensive documentation, tutorials, and community support. Premium users get access to priority support and one-on-one consultations.

Can I integrate with existing systems?

Yes! Our platform supports integration with popular APIs, databases, and third-party services. You can specify integration requirements in your natural language descriptions.
